Common Exercises

The following are common exercises that we encourage our clients to do as a part of their daily exercise routine.

Burpee
Burpee: These are great, full body exercise to increase strength and endurance. Begin in standing position, drop into a squat and extends your hands forward, kick your feet back and then forward again quickly, then jump up from a squatted position.
Planks
Planks: It build your core strength. To perform a plank, get in push up position and test your forearms on the floor. Hold the position as long as you can.
Mountain Climber
Mountain Climbers: These are good cardio exercise. Place your hand on the floor in a push up position then bring one knee up to your chest and switch as quickly as you can(as though you are climbing a mountain).
